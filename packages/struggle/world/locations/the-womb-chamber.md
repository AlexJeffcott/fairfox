# The Womb Chamber

## Description
Dark. Filthy. The grime is human — generations of residue layered on surfaces that are themselves intact. The technology works perfectly. It has always worked perfectly. It just looks ancient because people have been coming through it for a very long time.

The room is small and curved. One exit, leading into the corridor.

## Significance
This is where every generation begins. The reader never sees it — the story opens in the corridor, already moving. The womb is behind her, in the dark she glances back at but cannot return to.

## Story role
Backstory only. The womb exists in the world but not in the narrative. Its existence is implied by the corridor, the bones, the fact that she is here at all.
