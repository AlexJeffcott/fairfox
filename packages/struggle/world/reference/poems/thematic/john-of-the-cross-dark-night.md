# St John of the Cross — "En una noche oscura" (from *The Dark Night of the Soul*)

Written after nine months in a cell barely six feet wide.

---

*En una noche oscura,
con ansias, en amores inflamada,
¡oh dichosa ventura!,
salí sin ser notada,
estando ya mi casa sosegada.*

On a dark night,
with longings, in love inflamed,
O happy chance!
I went out unseen,
my house being now at rest.

---

Last stanza:

*Quedéme y olvidéme,
el rostro recliné sobre el Amado;
cesó todo y dejéme,
dejando mi cuidado
entre las azucenas olvidado.*

I stayed, lost to myself,
I laid my face against the Beloved's;
all ceased, and I let go,
leaving my cares
forgotten among the lilies.
