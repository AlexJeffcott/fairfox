# The Settlers

Subjects from prior generations who survived but got stuck. They found exploits — wrong turnings, side passages, places where they could access supplies without progressing further. They settled.

Reasons vary. Injury. Couldn't solve the next challenge. Fear. Found something or someone worth staying for. Just got tired. No single explanation.

They're encountered late in the story and are a relatively small part of it. Some might help the Subject. Some might stand in the way. Some might root for her. They are people, not archetypes.

The Subject may stop with them for a time.

Details to be developed as the story requires — not before.
